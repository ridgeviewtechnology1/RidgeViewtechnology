What We Do

Complete Event
Technology Services

We design, manage, and execute every technical element of your event — audio, video, lighting, staging, and beyond. One team. Total accountability.

01
Audio Production

Clear, powerful audio is the foundation of every successful event. We engineer complete audio systems from the ground up — designed for your specific venue, audience size, and program content. Our engineers handle system design, acoustic analysis, equipment specification, load-in, and full show operation.

From intimate boardroom presentations to large-format conference general sessions, we deliver consistent, intelligible audio that supports your message without drawing attention to itself.

Line Array Systems Digital Mixing Wireless RF Coordination Distributed Audio DSP Engineering In-Ear Monitoring Dante Networking Acoustic Analysis
02
Video & Projection

Visual impact drives audience engagement. We design and operate comprehensive video systems that transform your content into an immersive experience — from multi-screen projection to full LED wall installations and broadcast-quality camera systems.

Our video team manages signal flow, content playback, camera direction, and real-time switching with redundant systems in place to ensure zero failure during your event.

LED Wall Systems Laser Projection IMAG Camera Systems Video Switching Multi-Screen Design Content Playback Signal Routing Broadcast Integration
03
Lighting Design

Lighting shapes how your audience experiences every moment of your event. Our designers create lighting environments that reinforce your brand, direct attention, and elevate production value — from clean corporate looks to dynamic performance atmospheres.

We handle all aspects of lighting design, programming, rigging, and operation, with full pre-visualization available for large-format productions.

Intelligent Moving Lights DMX Programming Architectural Lighting Scenic Lighting LED Wash Systems Brand Color Matching Pre-Visualization Followspot Operation
04
Staging & Infrastructure

A well-engineered physical environment is the platform everything else builds upon. We design and install all staging and rigging infrastructure — from custom-height platforms to full truss grids — with safety and precision at every step.

Our rigging team is certified and experienced in both venue and ground-supported systems, coordinating closely with venue management to ensure compliance and structural integrity throughout the build.

Modular Stage Platforms Custom Truss Design Chain Motor Rigging Power Distribution Ground Support Load Calculations Venue Coordination Safety Compliance
05
Show Control

In complex productions, every system must communicate seamlessly. Our show control engineers design and operate the backbone that ties audio, video, lighting, and communications together — ensuring every cue fires at the right moment with zero ambiguity.

We build redundancy into every critical signal path, so a single point of failure never becomes a production failure in front of your audience.

Show Control Systems Timecode Sync Redundant Signal Paths Network AV (AV-over-IP) Cue Management Intercom Systems Event Networking System Integration
06
Live Production

Whether your audience is in the room or watching from anywhere in the world, we produce a broadcast-quality experience. We operate live streaming, virtual event platforms, and hybrid productions that deliver the same professionalism online as on-site.

Our production team manages encoding, streaming delivery, camera direction, and real-time graphics — extending your event reach without compromising production quality.

Live Streaming Hybrid Event Production Multi-Camera Direction Virtual Backgrounds Real-Time Graphics CDN Delivery Recording & Post Virtual Platforms
How We Work

Our Production Process

Every engagement follows a proven framework — structured to eliminate surprises and deliver consistent results across every event.

01

Initial Consultation

We begin by understanding your event objectives, audience, venue, and technical requirements. This shapes every decision that follows.

02

Site Visit & Technical Assessment

Our team visits your venue to evaluate infrastructure, power, rigging points, acoustics, and any logistical constraints before we begin designing.

03

Scope of Work & Proposal

We document all systems, labor, and deliverables in a detailed, transparent proposal — no hidden costs, no vague line items.

04

Pre-Production Planning

We develop your run of show, technical workflows, and production schedules. All stakeholders and vendors are coordinated well in advance.

05

System Design & Engineering

Audio, video, lighting, and signal routing systems are engineered in detail. Equipment is finalized, tested, and prepared before load-in begins.

06

On-Site Execution

Our team installs, tests, and operates every system. We provide real-time technical direction and troubleshooting throughout your event day.

07

Wrap & Post-Event Review

We strike all equipment, document production outcomes, and deliver a post-event summary with observations and recommendations for future events.
